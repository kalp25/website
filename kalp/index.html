OUR STORY

Established in year 2000, Barista Coffee Company is the pioneer of coffee culture in India. The Barista Café chain of espresso bars delivers a truly Italian coffee experience in warm, friendly and relaxed environment. It provides a comfortable place for people to unwind over interesting conversations and a hot cup of coffee. The Barista Coffee Company Limited has over 225 Barista Coffee Espresso Cafe and Barista Coffee Kiosks across India including its Franchise partners. Besides India, Barista Coffee also has cafés in locations across Myanmar and Nepal.
